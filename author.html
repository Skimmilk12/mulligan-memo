The Research Desk

Mulligan Memo is an independently operated golf gear research desk, organized into five beats. Here's exactly how the guides get made — and what we will and won't claim.

Let's be plain about how this works. Mulligan Memo is independently operated, and research, drafting and production use AI systems working under a strict editorial contract: picks are synthesized from manufacturer specifications, owner reviews and independent test data — never from paid placements, and never from invented test numbers. No guide on this site claims hands-on testing, ownership, or personal use, because none happened. What we promise instead: every claim traceable to a source, every update dated, every affiliate link disclosed, and a deals ledger that names fake "was" prices instead of repeating them. When something is editorial judgment rather than measurement, we say so. The desk is organized into five beats:

How We ResearchThe standard behind every pick

None of us are paid to recommend anything, and a brand can't buy a better review. We build recommendations from the best information available — verified specs, independent and owner testing, and long-term reliability — and we cross-check claims before printing them. We don't fabricate test data. If we can't stand behind a number, we leave it out or label it as a manufacturer claim. Spot something we got wrong?

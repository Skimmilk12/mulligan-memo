About Mulligan Memo

Honest golf gear advice for the rest of us — the weekend players, the slicers, the budget-minded, and anyone who'd rather spend money on green fees than on hype.

Est. 2026 / Play it as it lies

Mulligan Memo exists because shopping for golf gear is needlessly confusing. Every box promises more distance, more spin, more forgiveness — usually all at once. We cut through the marketing and tell you what actually matters for your game, your swing speed, and your budget.

Our PromiseWhat you can count on

  • No fabricated data. We never invent test numbers, fake prices, or made-up specs. If we can't verify a claim, we don't state it as fact — we tell you it's our opinion or a manufacturer's claim.
  • Honest about weaknesses. Every product has trade-offs. We name the compromises — the weak putter in a beginner set, the wind-y sound of a budget driver — instead of pretending everything is perfect.
  • Reader-first, always. We recommend gear only when the evidence and the trade-offs justify the price. Our picks are never paid placements, and a brand can't buy a better review.
  • Budget-aware. We believe most golfers overspend. We'll tell you when the cheap option is the smart one — and when it's worth paying up.
  • Dated updates, no silent edits. Every guide shows when it was last updated, and when we correct something substantive, the correction is noted on that guide itself.

"We'd rather lose a sale than recommend gear we don't believe in."

Want to know how the recommendations get made? See how the desk works — Mulligan Memo is independently operated, research and drafting use AI systems, and we explain exactly how every pick is researched.

How We Make MoneyAffiliate links, explained plainly

Mulligan Memo is reader-supported. Many of our buying guides contain affiliate links: if you click one and buy something, we may earn a small commission — at no extra cost to you. That's it. The commission never changes our recommendation; we pick the best gear for you first and find the link second. If you ever feel a recommendation was driven by a payout rather than your interests, we've failed at our one job.

Where to StartOur most popular guides

New here? A few good entry points: the beginner's club guide, our complete golf ball buyer's guide, and the surprisingly popular guide to grips for arthritic hands. Or just browse everything by category.

Say HelloGet in touch

Got a question, a correction, or a piece of gear you want us to cover? The best way to reach the desk is to join the Sunday Memo and reply to any issue — replies land on the desk and we read every one. Honest feedback makes the site better, and corrections are always welcome.

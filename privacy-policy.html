Privacy Policy & Affiliate Disclosure

Plain-English: how this site handles cookies, analytics, and affiliate links. Last updated June 2026.

The Short VersionWhat you need to know

Mulligan Memo is a golf gear content site. We don't ask you for personal information, and we don't sell or share data. We use standard website analytics to understand which guides are useful, and we use affiliate links to keep the lights on. That's the whole story; the details are below.

Affiliate DisclosureHow we make money

Mulligan Memo is reader-supported. Many of our guides contain affiliate links to retailers such as Amazon and other golf-equipment merchants. If you click one of those links and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. As an Amazon Associate (and a participant in other affiliate programs), we earn from qualifying purchases.

Affiliate commissions never change which products we recommend or the price you pay. We pick what we believe is the best gear first, and add the links second. Clicking an affiliate link may cause the retailer to place a cookie in your browser so they can credit the referral; that cookie is controlled by the retailer, not by us.

Analytics & CookiesWhat we measure

  • Google Analytics. We use Google Analytics (GA4) to see aggregate, anonymized traffic — which pages are visited, roughly where visitors come from, and what's popular. This uses cookies and may collect a truncated IP address. We do not use it to identify you personally.
  • Search tools. We use Google Search Console and Bing Webmaster Tools, which report aggregate search data; they don't track you individually on our site.
  • Retailer/affiliate cookies. When you click an outbound product link, the destination retailer or its affiliate network may set its own cookies. Those are governed by that company's privacy policy.

Your ChoicesOpting out

You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings, and you can opt out of Google Analytics with Google's opt-out browser add-on. Doing so won't break the site.

Other DetailsThe fine print

  • Information we collect directly: none required. We don't run accounts or store personal data you submit. (Any newsletter feature, if offered, will say clearly what it collects.)
  • Third-party sites: our links lead to other websites (retailers, manufacturers). We're not responsible for their content or privacy practices — review their policies before sharing information.
  • Children: this site is intended for a general adult audience and is not directed at children under 13.
  • Changes: we may update this policy as the site grows; the "last updated" date above will change accordingly.
  • Contact: questions about this policy? Reach out via our About page — we read everything.

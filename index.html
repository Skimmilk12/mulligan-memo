TO:The readers FROM:The research desk RE:How we pick, and what we ignore

We don't run a lab, and we won't pretend to. The method is plainer: we read the tests, the owner threads, and the spec sheets so you don't have to — then we name the trade-off.

  • Verified specs, from the sheet — never from the ad.
  • Independent test data, credited to the people who did the testing.
  • Owner feedback in the hundreds, one-star reviews read first.
  • Street prices sanity-checked before every update. MSRP is a work of fiction.

If a claim can't survive those checks, it doesn't make the memo. And when a pick changes, we date the change — no silent edits.
